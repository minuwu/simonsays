What is Simon Says?

Simon Says is a classic memory game. The game will show you a sequence of colors. Your task is to repeat the sequence by clicking the colored buttons in the correct order. Each round, the sequence gets longer. How far can you go?

How to Play

  • Press any key to start.
  • Watch the sequence of flashes and sounds.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the buttons.
  • If you make a mistake, the game ends and your high score is saved.
  • Try to beat your high score!
